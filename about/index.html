About Niladri

I have spent the last 10 years in live events of all forms, producing concert, weddings, and DJing events for audiences of all sizes. Simultaneously I have worked with children, finding creative and innovative ways to teach difficult concepts. Then through a few different events, it occured to me to combine the skills of teaching - creating tools to enhance one's ability to do something - and live events - creating a fun experience - and become a developer.
